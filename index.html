About Me

I am deeply passionate about using language (both human and digital) to build things and create solutions.

I am fascinated with the parallels and connections between human languages and computer programming languages. And in the future, these two will continue to grow closer together. I believe that human language learning can be greatly strengthened by using some of tools that software developers have been using. I enjoy building tools that leverage these new technologies to help people learn other languages.

I'm really into arts and language, as well as math and logic. After my arts degree, I recently finished a computer science degree as well. My bookshelf is full of books on language learning, math, generative grammar, formal syntax, and computational semantics.

You can find me and some of my projects on GitHub.
